Who are we?

We're students, probably just like you. Frustrated with the services provided on campus (overpriced, unreliable, slow), we decided to start 3D printing for other students affordably, reliably, and fast.

We offer transparent pricing, and ensure quality with every print - And when you're ready, we'll even help you find a printer to buy.

How It Works

We offer one color and one choice of material; White PLA.
01.

Request A Print

Start by requesting a print through our form, fill out details like contact info, model info, and finally upload your files you would like printed. 

02.

After you fill our form out, one of our team members will contact you within ~24 hours to finalize model details, ensure correct printing, and collect payment. 

03.

After payment is received, your parts get printed, and we'll arrange delivery at the Langford Architecture Building.

Pricing

Just like our services, our pricing is simple. The cost of your 3D print is based on the weight of the material (filament) used to create it. We charge $0.03 per gram of filament.

When you send us your 3D model, our software calculates how much material will be needed to print it. For example, if your design uses 100 grams of filament, your total cost would be:

100 grams x $0.03 =3.00

This way, you only pay for the exact amount of material used, making it fair and transparent. 

On average we save customers $10-$15 per print.

Refund or Reprint Policy

We'll take full responsibility if your received an incorrect or unsatisfactory print. We offer two options, either reprint the piece, or receive a full refund for the part.

Contact the team member who handled your print for information!

We guarantee a full refund or reprint policy, let us know what you would like!
